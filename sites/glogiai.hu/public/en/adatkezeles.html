2. What data do we process, for what purpose and on what legal basis?

a) Visiting the website — server logs

When you visit the site, the hosting provider's server may log technical data (IP address, time of the request, browser type). The purpose is the operation and security of the site.

  • Purpose: ensuring the site functions, operational security, preventing abuse.
  • Legal basis: the controller's legitimate interest [Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR].
  • Retention: according to the hosting provider's logging practice, typically a few weeks.

b) Loading fonts — Google Fonts

The site loads fonts from Google Fonts. On loading, your browser connects directly to Google's servers, so Google may learn your IP address. Google Fonts does not set a cookie for this.

  • Purpose: a consistent appearance of the website.
  • Legal basis: the controller's legitimate interest [Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR].

c) Signing up for the beta-launch notification

If you provide your e-mail address on the homepage for notification, we process it solely to inform you about the platform launch and early access.

  • Data processed: e-mail address.
  • Purpose: notification about the beta launch.
  • Legal basis: your consent [Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR], which you may withdraw at any time.
  • Retention: until consent is withdrawn, at the latest until the notification following the platform launch.

5. Rights of the data subject

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

  • request information about and access to the data we process about you;

You can send your request to gvm@gvmszallitmanyozas.hu. We will respond without undue delay, at the latest within one month.
