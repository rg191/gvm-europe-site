Cookie policy

Effective from: 18 June 2026

1. What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small data file that a visited website stores on the visitor's device. Cookies can help the site function, or they can serve to analyse visitor behaviour.

2. Does this website use cookies?

In its current introductory (landing) form, glogiai.hu does not store any cookies on the visitor's device and does not use any analytics or tracking tools (such as Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel). You do not need to give consent to cookies for browsing, because none are set.

3. Externally loaded content

To render its appearance, the site loads fonts from Google Fonts (operated by Google Ireland Ltd. / Google LLC). When a font is loaded, your browser establishes a connection with Google's servers, during which Google may learn the visitor's IP address. Google Fonts does not set a cookie for this. For more, see the Privacy Notice and Google's privacy policy.

4. Server-side logging

The hosting provider (Hetzner Online GmbH) may keep a technical log for the operation and security of the server, which may contain IP addresses and requests. This is not a cookie stored in the browser. Details in the Privacy Notice.

5. After the beta launch

Once the registration and login functions go live, the platform may use cookies strictly necessary for operation (session cookies) and — based on your prior consent — possibly analytics cookies. In that case we will update this policy and display the consent notice (cookie bar).

6. Managing cookies in your browser

You can manage or delete cookies at any time in your browser settings. As this site currently sets no cookies, there is nothing to do for now.

7. Contact

You can send questions about cookie handling to info@glogiai.hu.
